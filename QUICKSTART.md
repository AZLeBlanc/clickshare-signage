# Quick Start Guide

## For Marketing Team: Adding Images

1. Go to https://github.com/AZLeBlanc/clickshare-signage
2. Click into the `images` folder
3. Click **Add file** → **Upload files**
4. Drag your images and click **Commit changes**
5. Wait 1-2 minutes — done!

## Controlling Order

Rename files with numbers:
- `01-promo.jpg`
- `02-hours.jpg`
- `03-contact.jpg`

## Removing Images

1. Click on the image in the `images` folder
2. Click the trash icon (🗑️)
3. Commit

## Troubleshooting

**Images not showing?**
- Wait 2 minutes after uploading
- Hard refresh the page: `Ctrl + Shift + R`
- Check that filename ends in `.jpg`, `.png`, `.gif`, or `.webp`

**Need help?**
Contact IT or open an issue in this repo.
